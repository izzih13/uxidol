About Us

UX Idol is all about you, the participant! We want the experience to be as enriching and fulfilling as possible, so we've packed this event full of opportunities to learn, participate, and engage with your UX community.

Our Goals & Desired Outcomes

  • Cultivate and strengthen meaningful connections with UX, Product, Marketing, and Engineering Leaders in San Diego
  • Create a platform for junior level UX professionals to highlight their work, build confidence, and grow their career path
  • Showcase the undeniable value and unique abilities of junior level UX professionals, thus opening up more opportunities for them in the job ecosystem

Ensure You Are Successful at UX Idol

  • Spend quality time with judges - be fully present, engage in meaningful conversations, and take notes
  • Connect with the fabulous presenters to talk through their process, experiences, and lessons learned
  • Compose an expeditious action plan to elevate and level up
